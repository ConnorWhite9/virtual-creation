Joji

Joji is a Japanese American Artist who makes songs that blend together the genres of R&B, lofi, and hiphop.

He truly took off as an artist after the release of his first studio album Ballads 1 and has now amassed over 18.9 million monthly listeners on Spotify.

Slow Dancing In The Dark

Slow Dancing in the Dark was Joji's second single off the album Ballads 1 and was his first song to appear on the Billboard Top 100.

The song explores the confusion and conflict during a relationship and the worry that feelings may not be reciprocated. The song blends together R&B and lofi to create a truly spectacular melody.

Run

Run was Joji's second single off the album Nectar and was the biggest hit on the album

The song explores the idea of wanting to escape from a clearly unhealthy relationship. This song follows Joji's usual blend of genres but also incorporates some elements of alternative rock to give the song a unique appeal.

1AM Freestyle

1AM Freestyle was the last track on Joji's album Smithereens.
